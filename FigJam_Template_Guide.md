# Vision Stack FigJam Template Guide

This document outlines the visual structure and elements needed to build the accompanying FigJam board for the Vision Stack Workshop.

## Global Board Elements
- **Instructions Box**: At the top left, a brief summary of the workshop, rules of engagement (e.g., "Camera on, mic off during silent brainstorms"), and a link to the main Workshop Framework document.
- **Finished Stack Container**: A prominent column or box on the right side of the board where the finalized outputs of each section will be dragged/copied, creating the final "Vision Stack" from top to bottom.

---

## Section 1: Principles (45 mins)
**Visual Layout:**
- **Area 1: Diverge (Silent Brainstorm)**
  - A large workspace with a prompt: *"What specific behaviors do you value most in your colleagues?"*
  - A stack of blank sticky notes ready for use.
- **Area 2: AI Synthesis**
  - A large, clearly marked empty text block designated for the Facilitator labeled *"AI Generated Themes (Paste Here)"*.
- **Area 3: Voting & Alignment**
  - An area to drag the top themes. Include instructions for voting: *"Use +1 stamps to vote for the themes that resonate most."*
- **Area 4: The Final Cut**
  - 4-5 prominent boxes/cards where the finalized principle titles and 1-sentence descriptions will live.

---

## Section 2: Purpose (30 mins)
**Visual Layout:**
- **Area 1: The "Why" Prompts**
  - Three distinct columns or boxes, each with a stack of stickies:
    1. *Who do we help?*
    2. *What is their biggest struggle?*
    3. *How does our work change their lives?*
- **Area 2: AI Generation**
  - A large text block labeled *"AI Generated Purpose Statements (Paste Here)"*.
- **Area 3: Drafting & Finalizing**
  - A "sandbox" area where the team can drag down their favorite parts of the AI statements.
  - One large, highlighted "Final Purpose Statement" card.

---

## Section 3: Mission (40 mins)
**Visual Layout:**
- **Area 1: Mad-Libs Framework**
  - Pre-filled sticky notes (or text boxes) duplicated for everyone with the mad-libs formula:
    > *"We build `[solution/product]` for `[specific audience]` so they can `[primary outcome]`."*
- **Area 2: AI Refinement**
  - A text block labeled *"AI Synthesized Mission Options (Paste Here)"*.
- **Area 3: Debates & Alignment**
  - A dedicated "Debate Ring" area for adjusting the top choices.
  - One large, highlighted "Final Mission Statement" card.

---

## Section 4: Strategy (50 mins)
**Visual Layout:**
- **Area 1: Strategic Pillars Brainstorm**
  - A wide open area with suggested cluster headers horizontally aligned: *User Experience*, *Technical Foundation*, *Process*, *[Blank Header]*.
  - A stack of sticky notes.
- **Area 2: AI Devil's Advocate**
  - A text block labeled *"AI Critique & Risk Analysis (Paste Here)"*.
- **Area 3: Iteration & Hard Choices**
  - 3 large, distinct pillars (shapes or frames) labeled "Strategic Pillar 1," "Strategic Pillar 2," and "Strategic Pillar 3."

---

## Section 5: OKRs (40 mins)
**Visual Layout:**
- **Area 1: Objective Setting**
  - 2 large cards labeled *"Objective 1"* and *"Objective 2 (Optional)"*.
- **Area 2: Metrics Brainstorm**
  - An area beneath the Objectives with a prompt: *"How do we measure success for these?"* and a stack of stickies.
- **Area 3: AI Metrics Assistant**
  - A text block labeled *"AI Generated SMART Key Results (Paste Here)"*.
- **Area 4: Reality Check (Finalization)**
  - Beneath each agreed-upon Objective card, place 3 slots/cards for the finalized Key Results.
  - Place a sticky or text warning that reads: *"Reality Check: Do we have the tools/data to measure this TODAY?"*

---

## The Output (Wrap-up)
- **The Vision Stack Artifact**: Ensure the right-hand "Finished Stack Container" has a clean, polished layout. It should visually stack like a pyramid or layered cake:
  - **Top**: Principles (4-5 items)
  - **Layer 2**: Purpose (1 sentence)
  - **Layer 3**: Mission (1 sentence)
  - **Layer 4**: Strategy (3 pillars)
  - **Base**: OKRs (1-2 Objectives, 3 KRs each) 
- Add a space for "Next Steps" with the check-in date prominently displayed.
